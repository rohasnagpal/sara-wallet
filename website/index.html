Why Sara

Built for stablecoins. Not everything.

General-purpose wallets are built to do everything crypto can do — which means they hand you all of its complexity, too. Sara does one thing: move your dollars, safely.

Your keys, your funds

Sara is non‑custodial. It never holds, touches, or has access to your money — unlike a bank wire or remittance company sitting in the middle.

One clear preview, every time

Before anything is signed, Sara shows you exactly what will happen — swap, bridge, gas, all of it. No blind signing, no fine print to decode.

Contract addresses, not names

Anyone can launch a token called “USDC.” Sara only ever recognizes verified real contracts — a lookalike wearing the right logo won't fool it.

No approval graveyard

Sara handles token allowances precisely, so you're not left with dozens of forgotten permissions scattered across chains, years later.
